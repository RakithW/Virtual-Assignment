about me

Welcome to my About Me page! I'm thrilled to share my journey with you. As a dedicated student at Georgian College, I am passionately pursuing a career in computer programming. The realms of HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Python have become my playground, where I constantly explore and create. Beyond academics, I'm an avid gamer, finding joy in virtual adventures. When not immersed in code or virtual worlds, you'll find me in the great outdoors with my loyal companion, my dog Duke, who is undoubtedly the best walking buddy a person could ask for. Speaking of companionship, I have an exciting hobby of collecting hot wheels, a nostalgic and thrilling pastime that brings out the child in me.

After graduation, I hope to find a job where I can use my programming skills to create websites and games. Working on every line of code, my goal is to make the digital world more enjoyable and user-friendly. Bringing joy and value to users through my creations is what inspires me the most.

Some of my interests are cars, especially Japanese cars. This is also the reason why I want to travel to Japan - to see all the unique cars and learn more about the Japanese culture. Additionally, I have a desire to visit Korea, Singapore, and Ireland. As I pursue a career in programming, I envision myself coding and traveling the world with my laptop as that's all a programmer needs.

Thank you for taking the time to read about my passions, interests and aspirations. Whether it's creating captivating websites, crafting immersive games, or embarking on new adventures, I'm excited to embrace the future with an open heart and an insatiable curiosity.
